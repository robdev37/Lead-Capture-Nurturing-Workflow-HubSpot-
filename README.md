# 🚀 Lead Capture & Nurturing Workflow (HubSpot)

## 📌 Overview
This project showcases an end-to-end **lead capture and nurturing system** built using HubSpot.

It automates the entire process from form submission to follow-up — ensuring leads are captured, engaged, and handed off to sales without manual effort.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To build a streamlined workflow that:
- Captures leads from a form  
- Sends automated follow-up emails  
- Assigns leads to a sales owner  
- Creates tasks for timely human follow-up  
- Keeps all activities tracked inside the CRM  

---

## ⚙️ Workflow Summary
The automation follows a structured sequence:

1. Lead submits form (Trigger enrollment)  
2. Contact is assigned to an owner (round-robin)  
3. Contact record is created/updated in CRM  
4. Immediate confirmation email is sent  
5. Delay (allow engagement)  
6. Follow-up email #1 is sent  
7. Delay  
8. Final follow-up email is sent  
9. Task is created for sales follow-up  
10. Contact record is updated
